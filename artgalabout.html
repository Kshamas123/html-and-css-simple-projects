OUR HISTORY

Founded in 1990, our gallery has been a cornerstone of the local art community for over three decades. Our journey began with a small collection of contemporary art pieces, and over the years, we've grown to become one of the most respected art institutions in the region. We have hosted numerous exhibitions featuring both emerging and established artists, and our commitment to promoting the arts remains unwavering.
